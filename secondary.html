Awakening to the joy of Painting

Yatora knows Mori and see her paintings, this awakes something
in his hearth, but he refuses to accept it because he says art is
a waste of time, artist are a group of crazy people who will die
hungry without getting money for live a decent life.

One day after discuss with her classmate Yatora arrives home and
starts drawing, saying that it is a waste of time but for some reason he
can't stop drawing, making this event his awakening to the joy of painting.
